## The Temptation of Oneness: Comfortable Death

Why is "unity" so tempting?

Because unity means the **disappearance of damping**.

This sounds like heaven.

But thermodynamics tells us: **equilibrium state is death**.

A completely smooth, frictionless, difference-free system is a system of **maximum entropy**. It has no ability to do work, no potential for evolution.

Most matter in the universe (stones, water, air) has chosen unity. They follow the Second Law of Thermodynamics, lying flat in the comfort zone of low energy levels. They are the silent majority.
